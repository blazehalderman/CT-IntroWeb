Costa Rica

Blaze Halderman

My favorite place to visit is Costa Rica. It's lush green environment and the wildlife surrounding the area make it twice as enjoyable.

To be more specific, I like to visit the Arenal Volcano which is more in the northern area of Costa Rica.

During my visit to Costa Rica, I also was able to visit the beaches. The beach is most intense because of how hot it gets, but is most relieving when you enter the into the ocean.

Overall, I had a great time during my visit to Costa Rica and hopefully one day will return.
